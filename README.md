TwisterWall (The Wheel of Fun Wall)

Have you guys ever played or seen Twister?
It's a game played on a floor with colored dots where we have to use 4 parts of our body to hold on: Left Hand, Right Hand, Left Foot, and Right Foot, stretching to reach the spots. The fun is that in the next round, we randomly pick one body part to grab a random color, and keep going. So, I tried adapting this to rock climbing—specifically in the kids' zone (stealing kids' toys lol 55555)—turning it into a mini-game like Twister. That's why I call it TwisterWall.
